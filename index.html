Core Features

Built for Controller Dominance

Pure AI aimbot for controller players — no wallhacks, no ESP, just intelligent computer vision targeting that feels natural.

AI Aimbot Engine

YOLOv8-based detection with ONNX runtime. DirectML and TensorRT acceleration for maximum performance on any GPU.

StickTrackerX

Advanced aim tracking with configurable FOV, smoothness, prediction, and deadzone control for human-like aim.

StickyAxis Engine

PID-based smoothing with per-axis configuration, humanization curves, and native feel on any sensitivity.

Capture System

Flexible screen capture via mss, DXCAM, or capture card. Multi-monitor support and custom crop regions.

Triggerbot

Auto-fire with configurable delay, hitbox size, and activation modes. ADS-only, always-on, or hip fire.

Anti-Recoil

Per-weapon recoil compensation with adjustable intensity. Profile saves per game and weapon.
